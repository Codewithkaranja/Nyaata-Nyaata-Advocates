17+ Years of Legal Excellence

Founded in 2006, Mutinda and Musyoka Company Advocates has established itself as a premier law firm in Machakos and throughout Kenya. Our firm was built on the principle that exceptional legal service should be accessible, personalized, and results-driven.

Over the years, we have grown from a small practice to a respected firm with seven dedicated legal experts, each specializing in different areas of law to provide comprehensive legal solutions to our clients.

Our track record of successfully handling over 500 cases speaks to our commitment, expertise, and the trust our clients place in us. We take pride in our ability to navigate complex legal challenges and deliver favorable outcomes.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional legal services with integrity, professionalism, and a client-centered approach. We are committed to protecting our clients' rights and interests while upholding the highest standards of the legal profession.

Our Vision

To be the leading law firm in Eastern Kenya, recognized for our legal expertise, ethical practice, and commitment to justice. We aim to set the standard for excellence in legal representation and community service.

Meet Our Founding Partners

Adv. Mutinda

Senior Partner

With over 20 years of experience in litigation and corporate law, Advocate Mutinda brings strategic insight and formidable advocacy skills to every case. He holds an LLB from the University of Nairobi and was admitted to the Kenyan Bar in 2001.

Adv. Musyoka

Managing Partner

Specializing in commercial law and real estate, Advocate Musyoka has built a reputation for meticulous preparation and successful negotiation. He graduated with honors from Moi University School of Law and has been practicing since 2003.
