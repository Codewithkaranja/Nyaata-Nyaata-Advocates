Adv. Maingi Kisilu

Advocate of the High Court of Kenya

Maingi Kisilu Company Advocates is a premier Machakos-based law firm where Advocate Maingi Kisilu serves as a Managing partner, bringing over a decade of results-driven and client-focused legal practice.

Advocate Maingi Kisilu is an accomplished legal practitioner with extensive expertise in civil and commercial litigation, corporate advisory, property conveyancing, and family law matters. Her practice is characterized by meticulous attention to detail and a deep commitment to achieving optimal outcomes for clients.

Professional Background

Advocate Maingi Kisilu was admitted to the Kenyan Bar in 2014 and has since built a distinguished career representing clients across various sectors including banking, real estate development, manufacturing, and family enterprises. Her approach combines legal acumen with practical business understanding.
