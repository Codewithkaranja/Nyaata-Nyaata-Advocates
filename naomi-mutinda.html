Adv. Naomi Mutinda

Advocate of the High Court of Kenya

Musyoka & Mutinda Company Advocates is a premier Machakos-based law firm where Advocate Naomi Mutinda serves as a Managing partner, bringing over a decade of results-driven and client-focused legal practice.

Advocate Naomi Mutinda is an accomplished legal practitioner with extensive expertise in civil and commercial litigation, corporate advisory, property conveyancing, and family law matters. Her practice is characterized by meticulous attention to detail and a deep commitment to achieving optimal outcomes for clients.

Core Practice Areas

  • Corporate Advisory & Commercial Law
  • Conveyancing & Real Estate Transactions
  • Family Law & Succession Matters
  • Dispute Resolution & Arbitration
  • Legal Compliance & Regulatory Advisory
  • Contract Drafting & Negotiation
  • Debt Recovery & Insolvency

Professional Background

Advocate Naomi Mutinda was admitted to the Kenyan Bar in 2014 and has since built a distinguished career representing clients across various sectors including banking, real estate development, manufacturing, and family enterprises. Her approach combines legal acumen with practical business understanding.
